Package Delivery & Order Placement Methods

At Tipax, sending packages — both within the same city and across cities in Iran — is simple and flexible. Choose the method that best suits you.

MyTipax

Place your order easily via the MyTipax mobile app or website.

Fast Box Shipment Update

You can expect timely delivery with real-time tracking updates.

Contacting Branches

You can place an order by calling the central office.

Package Journey

1
Order Placement:

Start by placing your delivery request using one of our available booking methods.

2
Pickup:

Once your order is confirmed, we’ll arrange for your package to be picked up from the sender’s location.

3
Processing & Shipping:

Your package will be sorted and processed at our automated hubs, then shipped via ground or air transportation based on your destination.

4
Delivery:

The package will be delivered to the recipient’s address, a smart locker (available in Tehran), or the nearest Tipax branch.
